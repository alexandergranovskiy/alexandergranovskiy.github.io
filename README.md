# E-commerce Case Studies and Playbooks

Practical case studies and playbooks focused on measurable outcomes: growth, conversion, on-site search and merchandising, risk and payments, and automation.

## Featured case studies

### Order Risk Rules Engine - real-time screening to cut fraud and speed fulfillment

Pre-capture screening, IP geo and proxy checks, risk lists, payment pattern analysis, address validation, and ERP/fraud API signals with auto-release vs review routing.
Results: +2 to +4 pp approval rate, -15 to -30% chargebacks, -10 to -20% false declines.
[https://www.alexgranovskiy.com/case-study-order-risk-rules-engine/](https://www.alexgranovskiy.com/case-study-order-risk-rules-engine/)

### On-Site Search and Merchandising System - relevance, ranking, and controls

Relevance tuning, SEO-safe facets and zero-result fallbacks, inventory and margin-aware ranking, and operator-friendly merchandising controls.
Results: +8 to +15% search revenue, -20 to -40% zero-result rate, +5 to +12% add-to-cart from search sessions.
[https://www.alexgranovskiy.com/case-study-search-merchandising/](https://www.alexgranovskiy.com/case-study-search-merchandising/)

### Semantic Query Clustering Engine - SERP overlap clustering for ecommerce SEO

Cluster queries by shared SERP results to consolidate pages, reduce duplication and cannibalization, and strengthen topical authority.
[https://www.alexgranovskiy.com/case-study-semantic-query-clustering-engine/](https://www.alexgranovskiy.com/case-study-semantic-query-clustering-engine/)
